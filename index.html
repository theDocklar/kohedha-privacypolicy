1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services to you.

a. Personal Information

While using our App, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personal Information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Name: To personalize your experience.
  • Email Address: For account management, communication, and support.
  • Travel Preferences: Such as preferred destinations, activities, dietary restrictions, or accessibility needs, to tailor recommendations and support.
  • Payment Information: If you make purchases or bookings through the App (e.g., for tours, accommodations), we may collect payment details. This information is typically processed by third-party payment processors, and we do not store sensitive payment card details on our servers.
  • Authentication Information: If you choose to log in via third-party services (e.g., Google, Facebook), we may collect information provided by those services, such as your profile picture and public profile information.

b. Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how the App is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This Usage Data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our App that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

c. Location Information

As a traveling support agent, the App may require access to your device's location to provide relevant services, such as:

  • Real-time Navigation: To guide you to destinations.
  • Nearby Recommendations: To suggest attractions, restaurants, or services close to your current location.
  • Emergency Assistance: To quickly identify your location if you request help.
Important: We will only collect your location information with your explicit consent. You can enable or disable location services when you use our App at any time through your device settings.

d. Information from Third-Party Services

We may integrate with or use third-party services (e.g., booking platforms, mapping services, weather APIs) to enhance your travel experience. These third parties may collect information directly from you or share information with us in accordance with their own privacy policies.
